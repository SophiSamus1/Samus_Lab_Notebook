# Desiccation treatment

## Breakpoint temperatures of three ʻopihi across height index
### 'distcrust' in blue is the measured distance in cm of each ʻopihi to the crustose coralline algae. This gives us a reference for where the ʻopihi lived and how close they were to the water. In this group, the ʻopihi collected furthest from the crustose was the tallest and had the lowest breakpoint temperature. The ʻopihi collected closest to the crustose had the shortest shell.
