As a reminder, here is the schematic of the stress trials. 3 ʻopihi were randomly placed into thermal, desiccation, or control treatments and transplanted accordingly. Body, shell, rock, and air temperature, weight, distance to CCA, rock angle, compass heading, illuminance, and wind speed were measured before and after transplantation. 8 replicates for a total of 24 ʻopihi sampled.
